Incarnation in CORBA

It is a process of associating a servent with a CORBA obj as to recieve requests. This provides a serveant for Virtual form of CORBA Object. Lifetime of both servant abd objects are idependent.
Incarnate using - activate_object().
but the reverse is also possible, create_reference() activates an object without incarnating a servant, and servant incarnation is later done on demand with a Servant Manager.
